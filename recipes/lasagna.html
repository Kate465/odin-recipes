Lasagna Recipe


Description

Lasagna (or lasagne) is a classic Italian dish consisting of stacked layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, alternating with fillings and sauces. It is then topped with cheese and baked (al forno) until golden and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Lasanga noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna according to recipe intructions
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let lasanga rest before serving
